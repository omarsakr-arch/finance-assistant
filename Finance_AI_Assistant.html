Android Setup Guide

Use the AI Assistant anywhere on your phone

STEP 1

📱 Open Claude.ai on Android

Go to claude.ai in Chrome on your Android phone. Log in with your Anthropic account. For the best experience, tap the 3-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen" to install it like an app.

STEP 2

📌 Pin This Assistant

Start a conversation with this Finance Assistant page open. Bookmark it in Chrome. You can also save the HTML file to your phone and open it offline — all your data saves locally in the browser.

STEP 3

📷 Upload Receipts Directly

In the AI Chat tab, tap the camera zone (📷). Your phone will let you choose: Camera (take a new photo of a receipt), Gallery (pick a saved screenshot), or Files. The AI reads and extracts all expense data automatically.

STEP 4

🎤 Use Voice to Text

Tap the text box, then tap the microphone icon on your Android keyboard. Say: "Paid three hundred fifty EGP at Spinneys for groceries today" — the AI extracts the amount, merchant, category, and date automatically.

STEP 5

📸 Screenshot Bank Apps

After a bank transfer or credit card payment, take a screenshot and upload it here. The AI reads the amount, date, and merchant from the screenshot and creates the log entry. Works with CIB, NBE, HSBC, QNB apps.

STEP 6

📥 Export to Google Sheets

Go to the 📋 My Log tab → tap "Export CSV". Open the downloaded file → share it to Google Drive. In Google Sheets, use File → Import to paste new rows into your Finance Tracker. Or copy-paste the rows manually.

STEP 7

🔄 FX Rates on Mobile

In your Google Sheet's 💱 FX Rates tab, replace the rate values with =GOOGLEFINANCE("CURRENCY:USDEGP"). This auto-updates the rate every time you open the sheet on your phone — no manual updates needed.

STEP 8

⚡ Quick Tips for Daily Use

• Use Quick Entry tab for fast logging when the AI feels slow
• Set a daily reminder at 9pm to log the day's expenses
• After every Porsche trip ticket payment (40k EGP), log it immediately
• Take a photo of every physical receipt before discarding it
• Share shopping screenshots to this assistant from WhatsApp
